About us

Who are we?

We are 6 students from the 'Applied informatics' program from University College Ghent. Bavo Janssens, Jelle Destoop, Laurens van Bruwaene, Boris De Torck, Simon Vanhaverbeke and Joris Claeys.
For the course I1Talent we had to come up with an innovative idea that had to do with IT. Together we have established a so-called iRelive company and invented our first product, the VRelive glasses.


Our idea

The idea is to make the visitor experience a new way of tourism. In this way we can make the discovery of history more interactive! We do this by brining the past to the present. People will be able to explore buildings, cities, musea, plazas like they where in the past and make the comparison with today. In doing so you can request a virtual guide who will guide you in a unique way. Or relive an historic event like you where really there. And all that through our VRelive-glasses!

Our Product

VRelive

We have VRelive-glasses that will show the location where you are at exacly like it used to be in the past. There will be different time periods available. The number of available time periods is dependant of your exact location. It is possible to add persons from a historic period to the location. So you can get an authentic view of how they used to live in that time period.
The VRelive-glasses use VR-technology, but goes a step further. Bystanders will also be visible in the virtual space. We do this by scanning them. People with out VRelive-glasses will also be visible in the virtual world and in this way you can interact with each other. There is also the possibility to organise special events with our VRelive-glasses, where everybody wears our glasses. In this way you can fully immerse in a historic event.
We have made an app where you can find an overview of all the locations that are included in our VReliveservice. On the basis of this app, you can set al the parameters for your visit to the past. Extra possibilities range from people with a face restriction (ex. color blindness) to chosing your own clothing in the virtual space. Our glasses are perfectly able to combine with personal glasses.
Ever wanted to dine in the Imperial Rome, have breakfast in the ancient Egypt? Our VRelive-glasses make it possible! Go into a restaurant, chose the desired time period and experience the authentic kitchen from then. The cook will prepare a dish from the chosen time period and the setting of the restaurant will be changed virtually.


Can it go even further?

In addition to the VRelive-glasses we will provide a technology that brins the past and present a step closer to each other. It are transparent glasses that simply shows the reality when they are turned off. When turned on this glasses make it possible to for example to see the coliseum in its current state, but also with virtual additions. This makes it so that the building, in this case the coliseum is virtually reconstructed. Just like that you can experience the best of both worlds: the old and the new Rome. Both combined into one image.
